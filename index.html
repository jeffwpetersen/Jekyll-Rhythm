Rhythm & Design

Vertical rhythm is an obsesion for any designer that cares about typogrophy. This Jekyll theme is intended to provide a vertical rhythm example. The Plumber sass mixin is used to calculate the baseline alignment of webfonts. https://jamonserrano.github.io/plumber-sass. A great resource for visualizing vertical rhythm can be found at http://nowodzinski.pl/syncope. A great article on the need to modify the strict baseline gridd for good paragraph typogrophy is here... https://zellwk.com/blog/wrong-about-vertical-rhythm. I've modified plumber to give paragrahs an approprate bottom margin. A Sass mixin for visulizing baseline grids is included. @include baseline-grid(). Also included is the susy grid backgrounds function, @function susy-svg-grid().
